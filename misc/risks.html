Technology Risks

Although there are many advantages, using robotic prosthetics comes with several hazards and moral dilemmas. Economic disparity is one of the biggest obstacles. High-end prosthetic limbs can cost tens of thousands of dollars, which prevents many people from having access to these revolutionary technologies (Mouzo, 2022). User safety and device dependability are also at danger from technical problems such mechanical malfunctions, cyber vulnerabilities, and maintenance requirements (Vujaklija, Farina, & Aszmann, 2016).
