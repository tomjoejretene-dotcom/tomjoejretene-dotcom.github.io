Technology Choices

For users, medical experts, and society at large, the expanding area of robotic prosthetics offers a number of options and courses. Design type, purpose, accessibility, and personalization are options available to amputees (Herr, 2014). Policymakers and healthcare systems must make decisions about equal access, ethical regulation, and financing priorities. It is becoming more and more important to consider whether robotic improvements should just be used for medicinal purposes or if they should also be used for optional human augmentation (Caldwell, 2021).
