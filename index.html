The purpose of the homepage is to provide an overview and navigation, allowing visitors to quickly grasp the overall structure and main content of the site, and easily access the section they want. In addition, you should include a brief abstract of your project or topic, summarizing the Technology/Topic, Opportunities, Risks, and Choices, so that visitors can understand the focus of your work at a glance.

Artificial Body Parts: The Importance of Robotic Prosthetics

Example Abstract

People are constantly trying to improve upon the creative technological advancements. Bionics is one of these novel developments. The science of creating artificial systems that resemble living things via the use of robotics and artificial intelligence is known as bionics. Products like bionic prosthetics, which replace a person's missing limb, can be made using this technology..
